Lacademy for Students

Lacademy is designed to match K-12 students living in the greater Los Angeles county with local tutors. The best part: students will be able to receive help from Lacademy tutors free of charge.

Lacademy for Educators

Interested in expanding your outreach? Lacademy's matching algorithm pairs you with students in need of your help. Leave a positive impact on students in need with Lacademy.

Our Mission

Lacademy was originally created to address the issue of students lacking access to academic support, particularly students from underrepresented, low-income communities. Our goal is to ensure that all Lacademy students will be able to find the help they need to succeed.
